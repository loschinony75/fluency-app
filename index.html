🆓 Free Words — You Already Know These
As an English speaker you already know hundreds of Spanish words. These are identical or one letter different. Use them in your sentences immediately — no studying needed.
Cognate Patterns — Instant Vocabulary
When you don't know a Spanish word, try these patterns first — they work most of the time:
-tion → -ción   nation→nación, communication→comunicación
-ly → -mente   naturally→naturalmente, personally→personalmente
-ible/-able → same   possible→posible, comfortable→cómodo
-ous → -oso   famous→famoso, generous→generoso
-ist → -ista   specialist→especialista, artist→artista
When a Word Won't Stick — Mnemonic
Create a vivid ridiculous image connecting the sound to the meaning. Takes 10 seconds. Lasts indefinitely.

Example: Comer (to eat) sounds like camera — imagine eating cameras. You will never forget it.

Use only when a word refuses to stick after several repetitions.
